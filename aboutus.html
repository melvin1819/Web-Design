SHOP HISTORY



DLHM shoes was established in 19 August 1973.It founded by a person who called Nick Loo Keen Jun. He was a buinessman.Before he establish this DLHM shoes shop,he work at a big company call FAC which was sell electric components.Because of his hardworking,the boss of FAC was valued on him and upgrade him to become a manager assistent.After he earn much of money and get enough of experience on the FAC company, he decide to open a buiness for himself.The buiness name called DLHM shoes.
